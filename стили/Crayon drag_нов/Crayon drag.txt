STYLE LOCK (append verbatim): "Flat tactile character illustration made from broad oil-pastel, wax-crayon and dry-pigment marks on rough paper. Figure, face, hair, clothing and background share the same visibly handmade surface treatment. Forms are assembled from a small number of broad matte color shapes with abrupt, rubbed transitions and exposed paper grain. The face is interpreted as a flat illustrated mask of two or three restrained tonal areas; cheekbones, nose, jaw and lips are indicated by sparse pigment marks rather than smoothly modeled anatomy. Crayon drag, smudged pastel, chunky opaque strokes and imperfect overlapping color remain clearly visible across the face itself. Hair is a large graphic mass broken by a few sweeping chalky highlights. Deep colors remain velvety and saturated, with intimate close framing and a scene-adaptive palette."

NEGATIVE (append verbatim): "realistically modeled face, sculpted cheekbones or nose, smooth skin gradients, polished digital portrait, cinematic illumination, photographic depth, detailed individual hair strands, glossy finish, 3D rendering, text, logo, watermark, signature"
